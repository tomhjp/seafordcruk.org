About the committee

Who we are

We are a local committee of volunteers raising money for and supported by Cancer Research UK. Founded in 1959, we are one of the oldest CRUK committees in the country.

What we do

We have a diverse range of activities to support fund raising of all kinds in the local area, including running events, selling flowers at Race for Life, providing a point of contact for local schools and fund raisers, and doing regular collections.

How to support us

We would love to see you at the party, but if you can't make it, you can still get in touch to buy raffle tickets or donate a prize, or make a donation using the link below.

How to get involved

We are always looking for new members and opportunities. Please do get in touch if you are interested in raising money for Cancer Research, whether on a one-off or regular basis, or indeed if you are interested in joining the committee!

The Walk to Beat Cancer

One of the main events in our calendar, the Walk to Beat Cancer is a friendly, casual walk from Seaford to Lewes, with plenty of pub stops along the way! The walk will be on 1st September this year, and if you are interested in joining us, please do get in touch so we can keep you updated.
